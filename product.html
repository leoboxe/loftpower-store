Will it actually support my 90 lb dog?+

Yes. The XL uses a dense, high-resilience orthopedic foam core rated for large and giant breeds — it holds its shape under heavy dogs and won't bottom out to the floor like crumb-filled beds.

Does the cover really wash clean?+

Completely. Unzip the plush cover and machine wash cold, tumble dry low. It comes out soft and fresh. The included waterproof liner keeps the foam itself bone-dry underneath.

How firm is it?+

Supportive-firm — the sweet spot for joints. Soft enough to be cozy, firm enough that hips and elbows are cushioned off the hard floor. It softens slightly to your dog's body over the first few nights.

Then you don't keep it. You get 120 nights to try it risk-free — if it's not right, we refund you in full and pay the return shipping. No restocking fees, no hassle.
